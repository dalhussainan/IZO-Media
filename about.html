About.

من أنا
// DHARI.ALHESINAN KW · 2026
// INTRO · تعريف

A director with a / camera,
a point of view,
and a deadline.

I'm Dhari Alhesinan — founder, director, and cinematographer at IZO Media.

For six years, I've been building visual stories for banks, galleries, artists, and brands across Kuwait and the GCC. What started as a one-person operation with a single camera has become a studio that punches above its weight — working with clients like Warba Bank, NBK, and Boubyan, while still caring about every frame like it's the first one.

I believe a film doesn't need a big crew to feel big. It needs a clear point of view, obsessive attention to craft, and someone who loves the work enough to stay up editing until it's right.

ظهري، مصور، ومخرج من الكويت. أسست IZO Media في 2020، وخلال ست سنوات اشتغلت مع بنوك، معارض فنية، فنانين، وشركات في الكويت ودول الخليج. عندي اقتناع واحد: الفيديو الحلو مب محتاج فريق ضخم، محتاج رؤية واضحة واهتمام بكل تفصيل.
